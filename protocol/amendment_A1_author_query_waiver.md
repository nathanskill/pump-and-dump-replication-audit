# Protocol Amendment A1 — Author Query Waived

> Date: 2026-07-22. Applies to `locked_protocol_v0.2.md` as frozen at commit
> `23089ce` (`23089ceebd93eed421b8a2a710135851dcab536e`). This amendment
> changes claim boundaries only. No outcome definition, threshold, split,
> model, or metric of the frozen protocol is modified; the freeze stands.

## Change

The Analysis-discipline clause anticipating an upstream author
artifact/label-provenance query is replaced by the following:

1. No author correspondence is undertaken for this manuscript, and none will
   be represented as attempted.
2. Every claim that depended on author clarification remains **permanently
   suspended** for this manuscript. In particular, the study makes no
   statement about the existence, content, or availability of any paper-era
   artifact, and no statement about how the manual `gt` labels were generated
   beyond what the public repository itself documents.
3. The Table III discrepancy is reported strictly descriptively: *"we were
   unable to recover the published Table III values from the currently public
   artifact alone; the paper-described parameters, the released code and the
   post-paper released matrices differ, and we document these differences
   without adjudicating their source."* The wordings `failed replication`,
   `not reproducible`, `[¬Re]`, and any claim adjudicating the original
   authors' results remain prohibited.
4. The submission is scoped as a **partial replication of the released
   artifact** (code-level result resolved, paper-level traceability gap
   documented) plus the evaluation-sensitivity audit. The audit carries the
   evidential weight that author correspondence would otherwise have carried.
5. The conservative redistribution boundary is unchanged: no upstream data
   re-upload; reviewers acquire inputs from the original public repository at
   the pinned commit. Any redistribution question that arises at submission
   is directed to the venue's editors, not resolved by assumption.
6. If a reviewer or editor requests author contact during review, that
   request will be handled at that time and documented; this amendment only
   removes the pre-submission query.

## Acknowledged cost

Venue-fit risk at replication-focused venues increases: a reviewer may ask
why the authors were not contacted. The mitigations are the strict partial-
replication scoping (point 4), the completed robustness audit, and full
artifact/hash documentation. This trade-off was accepted by the author on
2026-07-22.
